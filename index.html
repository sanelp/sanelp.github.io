Features

Everything in one place

Built for people who play and collect One Piece TCG. No subscription, no account required.

📷

Card Scanner

Point your camera at any One Piece TCG card and it reads the code automatically. Works even with partial OCR errors — O vs 0, I vs 1.

📦

Binder System

Organize cards into named binders. Browse by set and see a live completion bar showing how close you are to completing each set.

🃏

Deck Builder

Build and validate tournament-legal decks. Includes opening hand simulator, text export, and deck duplication.

💰

Live Prices

Market prices synced live to every card. See the total value of your collection and open Cardmarket directly from any card.

🏆

Meta Tracker

Current tournament standings and top deck lists from Limitless TCG. Check what's performing before your next event.

🔄

Borrow Tracker

Track cards lent to or borrowed from friends. Grouped by person, with copy counts and return status.

♥

Wishlist

Save cards you're looking for and set a target price. Get a visual alert when the price drops below your target.

📊

Collection Stats

Breakdown by color, type, rarity, and value. Set completion percentages and total card counts across all your binders.

Live Prices

Know what your cards are worth

Prices are pulled live from OPTCG API and cached on your device. Every card in your collection shows its current market value, and you can jump straight to Cardmarket to buy or sell.

Set a target price on any wishlist card and the bell icon turns green when it drops below that number.

Borrow Tracker

Stop forgetting who has your cards

Log every card you've lent to or borrowed from someone. Grouped by person, with the option to mark cards as returned.

It sounds simple but it's genuinely useful when you're trading at locals.

Collection

Track your set completion

Every set shows a completion bar so you can see at a glance which sets you're close to finishing. Filter by set, sort by rarity or name, and scan in new cards directly from the collection view.

3,900+ cards bundled locally — works fully offline once downloaded.
